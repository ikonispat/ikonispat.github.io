How It Works

1. Vehicle Acquisition

We accept ELVs from private owners, insurance companies, and enforcement agencies with proper documentation.

2. Dismantling

We inspect vehicles, drain fluids, and remove reusable and recyclable parts.

3. Shredding

The vehicle body is crushed and shredded. Materials are separated via magnets and air classification.
