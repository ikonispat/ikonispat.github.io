About Us

Ikon Ispat Pvt. Ltd. was incorporated on 17th September 2024 to address the growing need for environmentally responsible dismantling of End-of-Life Vehicles (ELVs). We are a government-authorized scrap yard focused on sustainability, circular economy, and compliance.

Our facility uses both capital and labor-intensive techniques to provide world-class dismantling and material recovery solutions.
